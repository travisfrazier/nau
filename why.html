Quality and affordable education

Invest in yourself – the high-quality online education you receive through NAU can quickly pay for itself. With the lowest-priced tuition and highest accreditation in Arizona, an NAU education degree is one of the best values in finding an affordable path to advance your career.

There are several types of financial aid available to you:

  • Loans - Federal or private loans must be repaid and accrue interest over time.
  • Grants - Federal (e.g. Pell Grant) or state grants are awards that do not need to be repaid.
  • Scholarships - Private financial aid awards that do not need to be repaid.

If you need help navigating the funding options available, contact us using the phone number or the form above and we will be happy to answer any of your questions.

Accreditation

NAU is accredited by The Higher Learning Commission (HLC) of the Northern Central Association (NCA) of Colleges and Schools, one of six regional associations recognized by the Department of Education that accredits institutions of higher education.

Learning from where you are

No matter where you work or live, NAU makes your education a reality with flexible online degrees that you can earn at home. You can complete each course in as few as eight weeks, so you’ll be ready to put your degree to work sooner.

Expert faculty with practical experience

Our Master of Education programs are taught by expert faculty with years of experience. Our professors are passionate about your success both in and outside the classroom. They’ll challenge you professionally and personally to give you the skills and critical thinking that it takes to make a difference.
