Master of Education in Educational Leadership

You'll earn the skills you need to succeed in today's educational institutions through our Educational Leadership program. We offer three Educational Leadership tracks so you can choose the best option for you. All three tracks are offered 100 percent online and require 30-36 units to earn your degree.

Community College / Higher Education

This track will prepare you to inspire and transform teams to meet the challenges and opportunities of todayâ€™s higher education institutions.

K-12 Education

If you're an educator or other school personnel, this track will enable you to serve in K-12 educational leadership positions that do not require building or district certification.

Principal K-12 with Certificate

This track will prepare you to serve as a K-12 school principal in positions that do not require building or district certification.

Master of Education in Reading with Endorsement

This program will prepare you to meet the state standards as a reading specialist. You will learn the necessary framework for augmenting knowledge and skills related to teaching K-8th grade. You will also have the opportunity to pursue advanced courses in curriculum and instruction, educational psychology, educational foundations, and educational research. This program is TEACH Grant approved.

  • 30 units
  • 10 classes
  • 100% online

Master of Education in ESL (English as a Second Language)

30 percent of Arizona students speak a language other than English at home. The Master in ESL will prepare you for a career as a second language classroom teacher, ESL resource teacher, or a teacher responsible for providing ESL instruction and support in the regular classroom or special settings. You will gain the knowledge, skill, and disposition for involving community and families as partners and for developing ESL aptitude across reading, writing, and listening. This program is TEACH Grant approved.

  • 30 units
  • 10 classes
  • 100% online

Master of Education in Educational Technology

This program offers two areas of emphasis: Adult Learning and Training or K12 Teacher. You will gain the expertise and skills to integrate technology into your own teaching, model effective use of current technologies in diverse learning environments, and assist instructors in designing technology-enhanced learning experiences. This course is designed for educators who would like to become educational technology leaders within their school, district, or other professional setting, this course will provide you an opportunity to develop expertise and and skills in integrating educational technologies within any learning environment: from pre-kindergarten to college and from in-person to distant-learning classes.

  • 30 units
  • 10 classes
  • 100% online

Master of Education in Human Relations

The Master of Education in Human Relations is a theoretical, non-practitioner program designed to deepen your understanding of human behavior, the learning process, and interpersonal communication skills. This theoretically oriented plan is appropriate if you are in the business world, the military, or education, and are seeking advanced preparation in facilitating an understanding of human behavior and the learning process.

  • 30 units
  • 10 classes
  • 100% online
